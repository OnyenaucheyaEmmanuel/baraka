About

My name is Barakat Abiola Shuaib, I am from Malete, Kwara State. I am the second eldest of seven children in my family. From 1999-2008, I recieved my Basic School Leaving Certificate at Bright Pearl School, Ijesha, Surulere, Lagos and Primary School Leaving Certificate at The Rock Nursery & Primary School, Yebade, Sango-Ota, Ogun State. I later pursued Secondary Schooling at Federal Government Girls College, Omu-Aran, Kwara State and Future Leaders Comprehensive High School, Agbado, Lagos State, where I graduated with my Senior Secondary Certificate in 2009-2015. After that, I continued to the University of Ilorin, where I studied for my B.Ed in Adult Education Studies from 2015 - 2019.
